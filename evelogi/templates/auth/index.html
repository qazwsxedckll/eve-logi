Track your profits and capture the market! EVE Tycoon is a profit tracking and market management application for EVE Online. Track trade and industry profits across all your characters, so you can be sure you're making a profit! For industrialists, EVE Tycoon tracks your production lines so you can see exactly how much you spent to build an item. Traders can keep their market orders up to date with the order update hotkey, which opens the in-game market with the undercut price in the clipboard.
